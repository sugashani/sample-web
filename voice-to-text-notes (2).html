How VoiceNote works

From microphone to structured notes in seconds ‚Äî here's the full pipeline powering your voice sessions.

01

Capture Audio Input

The browser's Web Audio API captures your microphone stream in real time, chunking audio data into small packets for continuous processing.

Web Audio API
02

Stream to Amazon Transcribe

Audio chunks are sent over a WebSocket connection to Amazon Transcribe's streaming endpoint, which returns partial and final transcripts as you speak.

Amazon Transcribe WebSocket
03

Authenticate & Secure with Amplify

AWS Amplify handles authentication (Cognito) and securely signs requests to AWS services ‚Äî no credentials exposed on the client side.

AWS Amplify Cognito
04

Summarize with AI

Once the session ends, the full transcript is processed by an AI model to generate a concise summary, key points, and action items automatically.

LLM Summarization
05

Save & Sync Notes

Transcripts and summaries are saved to a cloud datastore via Amplify DataStore, making them instantly available across all your devices.

Amplify DataStore DynamoDB
